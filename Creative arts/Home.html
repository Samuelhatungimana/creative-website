MISSION

Nuturing a generation of globally talented refugees. Kakuma Creative Arts provides a space for youth talented refugees to showcase,and develop their God given talents. We strive to encourage youth refugees to harness their talent and learn how to use them to earn a living.

VISION

Raising our voices through arts to let the world know that we exist

INSPIRATION

Hardwork beats talent if talent doesn't work hard

About us

Kakuma Creative Arts is a community based organization that was spearheaded by three young men ready to serve their community by creating a space where the young talented youths in Kakuma refugee camp will have an opportunity to showcase their talents. TEST COMMIT
